WIKI

Microsoft Plus! For Kids was the second ever release of Plus! It was released in 1997 It includes three new applications: Talk It!, a text-to-speech program that says what users type using various voices; Play It!, an electronic keyboard with music and sound effects; and Paint It!, a version of Paint oriented for kids. It also contained new fonts, 10 new desktop themes, and Protect It!, parental controls for Internet Explorer.
