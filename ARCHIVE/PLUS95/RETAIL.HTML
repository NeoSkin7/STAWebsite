WIKI

Microsoft Plus! 95 (Retail) was the first release of Plus! 95. It included Space Cadet Pinball, DriveSpace 3 and Compression Agent disk compression utilities, theme support along with a set of 12 themes, dial-up networking server, dial-up scripting tool, and the graphical improvements such as anti-aliased screen fonts, full-window drag, the ability to stretch or shrink the wallpaper to fit the screen and highcolor icons.
